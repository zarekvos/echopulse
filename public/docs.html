Echo Pulse Documentation

Complete guide to mastering the Web3 echo detection game

What is Echo Pulse?

Echo Pulse is a cutting-edge Web3 game that combines stealth gameplay with blockchain technology. Players hunt invisible enemies using advanced echo detection systems while earning real cryptocurrency rewards and NFTs.

Core Concept

In Echo Pulse, enemies are completely invisible to the naked eye. You must use your cyberpunk robot's echo pulse system to reveal their locations temporarily, then strike before they disappear again.

Key Features

  • Stealth Combat: Hunt invisible enemies using echo detection
  • Multiple Weapons: Three distinct weapon types with unique characteristics
  • Web3 Integration: Earn cryptocurrency and NFT rewards
  • Progressive Difficulty: Increasingly challenging levels with smarter AI
  • Global Leaderboards: Compete with players worldwide
  • Cyberpunk Aesthetics: Immersive futuristic visual design

Installation

Get started with Echo Pulse in minutes

Step 1: Install MetaMask

Echo Pulse requires MetaMask to handle Web3 transactions and wallet connectivity.

1. Visit https://metamask.io
2. Click "Download" for your browser
3. Add the extension to your browser
4. Create a new wallet or import existing one
5. Save your recovery phrase securely
Security Warning

Never share your recovery phrase with anyone. Store it offline in a secure location. Anyone with access to your recovery phrase can control your wallet.

Step 2: Configure Network

Add the supported blockchain networks to your MetaMask wallet:

Level 1 Walkthrough

Starting Position

Remember: Enemies Are Invisible!

When the level starts, you won't see any enemies. This is normal! Use your echo pulse (SPACE) to reveal them.

Step-by-Step First Level

  1. Press and hold SPACE to emit your first echo pulse
    • You'll see enemies briefly highlighted in purple
    • Count how many enemies you spotted
    • Note their positions relative to walls and corners
  2. Select Pulse Gun (Press 1) - it's the most beginner-friendly
    • Good damage and high fire rate
    • Forgiving for new players
  3. Move towards the nearest enemy using WASD keys
    • Keep some distance - don't get too close
    • Use short echo pulses to track enemy movement
  4. Line up your shot and click to fire
    • Lead your target if the enemy is moving
    • Fire multiple shots for better hit chance
  5. Repeat for all enemies in the level
    • Level 1 typically has 3-5 Basic Stalkers
    • They move slowly and predictably

First Game Tips

Essential Survival Tips

Tip Why Important How To
Keep Moving Enemies hurt on contact Never stand still, use WASD constantly
Manage Energy Echo pulses consume energy Short pulses, not continuous holding
Use Walls Enemies can't pass through Put walls between you and enemies
Stay Calm Panic leads to mistakes Take time to aim, breathe

Common Beginner Mistakes

  • Holding SPACE too long: Wastes energy quickly
  • Standing still while aiming: Makes you an easy target
  • Rushing into groups: Take on enemies one at a time
  • Not switching weapons: Each weapon has optimal use cases
  • Ignoring health/energy bars: Monitor your status constantly

Echo Detection System

Master the core mechanic of invisible enemy detection

How Echo Detection Works

The echo system is the heart of Echo Pulse gameplay. Your cyberpunk robot emits sound waves that bounce off invisible enemies, briefly revealing their locations.

Echo Mechanics

  • Energy Cost: Each echo pulse consumes 20 energy points
  • Cooldown: 1 second between pulses
  • Range: 300 pixel radius from player position
  • Duration: Enemies remain visible for 3 seconds
  • Regeneration: Energy regenerates at 1 point per 0.1 seconds

Visual Indicators

Echo Wave Animation

When you send an echo pulse, you'll see expanding circular waves emanating from your robot. These represent the sound waves spreading outward.

Enemy Revelation

Enemies caught in the echo wave will fade into visibility with a red glowing outline. They gradually fade back to invisibility as the effect wears off.

Energy Bar

Your energy level is displayed in the bottom-left UI. The cyan-colored bar shows current energy, with a lightning bolt icon.

Strategic Echo Usage

Timing Your Pulses

Optimal Timing

Don't pulse randomly. Listen for audio cues like footsteps or enemy attacks. Use pulses when you suspect enemies are nearby or when entering new areas.

Energy Conservation

  • Wait for energy to regenerate between pulses
  • Use the 3-second visibility window effectively
  • Avoid panic-pulsing when surrounded
  • Plan your pulses based on enemy movement patterns

Area Scanning

Move around while pulsing to scan large areas. The echo range is limited, so positioning is crucial for effective detection.

Advanced Echo Techniques

Predictive Pulsing

Learn enemy movement patterns and pulse where you predict they'll be, not where they currently are.

Corner Clearing

Use echoes to safely check around corners and obstacles before moving into dangerous areas.

Combat Pulsing

During combat, pulse when enemies are likely to reposition or when you lose track of their movements.

Energy Management Warning

Running out of energy in dangerous situations can be fatal. Always keep at least 40 energy in reserve for emergency detection.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions

Wallet Connection Issues

MetaMask Not Detected

Wallet Not Found

Solution: Ensure MetaMask extension is installed and enabled. Refresh the page after installation.

Connection Hanging

1. Check MetaMask popup blocker
2. Disable other wallet extensions
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try incognito/private browsing mode
5. Restart browser completely

Wrong Network

If you're connected to the wrong blockchain network:

  1. Open MetaMask
  2. Click network dropdown
  3. Select Ethereum Mainnet or Polygon
  4. Refresh the game page

Game Performance Issues

Low Frame Rate

  • Close unnecessary browser tabs
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
  • Lower your screen resolution
  • Use hardware acceleration if available
  • Update your graphics drivers

Audio Problems

Audio Issues

Echo Pulse relies on audio cues. Ensure your browser has permission to play audio and your volume is appropriately set.

Gameplay Issues

Controls Not Responding

  1. Click on the game canvas to focus
  2. Check for sticky keys or accessibility features
  3. Try refreshing the page
  4. Test with a different keyboard

Echo System Not Working

Debug checklist:
 Energy bar above 20 points
 No cooldown timer active
 Canvas has focus
 Spacebar key functioning
 Visual effects enabled

Transaction Failures

Insufficient Gas

If blockchain transactions fail due to gas issues:

  • Increase gas price in MetaMask
  • Wait for network congestion to decrease
  • Switch to Polygon for lower fees
  • Ensure sufficient ETH/MATIC for gas

Nonce Errors

Nonce Issues

Solution: Reset your MetaMask account. Go to Settings > Advanced > Reset Account. This clears transaction history but doesn't affect your funds.

Base Network Integration

Why Base Network?

Optimized for Gaming

Echo Pulse leverages Coinbase's Base network for ultra-low transaction fees, fast confirmations, and seamless user experience perfect for gaming applications.

Base Network Advantages

  • Ultra-Low Fees: Transactions cost ~$0.001-0.01, perfect for frequent gaming interactions
  • Fast Confirmations: 2-second block times ensure instant reward distribution
  • Ethereum Compatibility: Full EVM compatibility with robust tooling
  • Coinbase Integration: Seamless onboarding for Coinbase users
  • Growing Ecosystem: Active DeFi and gaming community

Base Network Interactions

Adding Base to MetaMask

Quick Setup

Echo Pulse automatically prompts users to add Base network when connecting their wallet. Manual setup instructions below:

  1. Open MetaMask extension
  2. Click network dropdown (usually shows "Ethereum Mainnet")
  3. Click "Add Network" at the bottom
  4. Enter the Base network details from the table above
  5. Click "Save" and switch to Base network

Getting Base ETH

  • Coinbase: Direct withdrawal to Base (no fees for Coinbase users)
  • Bridge from Ethereum: Use official Base bridge (bridge.base.org)
  • Cross-chain DEX: Swap from other networks via Across or Hop
  • Centralized Exchanges: Some exchanges offer direct Base withdrawals

Gameplay Questions

How do I see invisible enemies?

Echo Pulse System

Press and hold SPACE to emit an echo pulse that temporarily reveals all enemies in the area. Manage your energy wisely as pulses consume power.

What's the difference between Guest and Wallet modes?

  • Guest Mode: Play for free, earn regular scores, compete on leaderboards
  • Wallet Mode: Connect crypto wallet, earn 1.5x score multiplier, receive ECP tokens and NFT rewards
  • Both modes offer the complete gameplay experience

Web3 & Crypto Questions

Do I need cryptocurrency to play?

Free to Play

No! Echo Pulse is completely free to play in Guest mode. Connecting a wallet only adds bonus rewards.

Which wallets are supported?

  • MetaMask (Recommended)
  • Trust Wallet
  • Coinbase Wallet
  • WalletConnect compatible wallets
