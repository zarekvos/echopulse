The Echo Pulse Story

The Invisible Threat

In the year 2157, humanity discovered that we were not alone. Alien entities had been living among us for decades, completely invisible to the human eye and most conventional detection methods.

These beings weren't necessarily hostile, but their hidden presence created fear and uncertainty. Traditional military equipment was useless against enemies that couldn't be seen.

The breakthrough came when Dr. Elena Vasquez developed the Echo Pulse Detection System - a revolutionary technology that could reveal invisible entities using advanced sound wave analysis.
